Release!!!!!

first of all, you need python installed, then you open NameGenerator.py or NameGenNoconsole 
then you choose the length of the name, 1 is a single syllable, 2 is more interesting, it
will create names of two syllables, I do recomend the size of 3 or 2, they create better results.
then you click the button "Generate" and boom, you have a random generated name.

you can choose and configure the file "syllables.txt", this will have something like this:
a
e
2mir
1ta
0li
tre

don't be scared, this are the syllables, you can delete or add the ones you like
the 0, 1 and 2s are the way the program chooses the position of the syllable.

0 - the syllable only appears at the beggining.
1 - the syllable only appears at the middle.
2 - the syllable only appears at the end.

when there is no number at the beggining of the syllable the program
places the syllable anywhere.
